Our expertise

Majesty is an engineering company that enjoys an impeccable reputation in the developer community and actively participates in application of IT to the educational system.

We build LMS, digital diaries, ERPS, and CRM systems for educational institions, utilizing the power of functional programming and the latest web technologies.

Learning Managment
System

LMS are projects for remote educator-led training. Managed learning environments track the progress of students and provide a more individualized approach. Majesty develops LMS that can be used by educational institutions as well as companies and enterprises internal training and progress management.

  • Learning content management systems provide space for creating, editing, and evaluating various learning materials like online tests and video courses.
  • Majesty can also build software for corporate learning that contains courses from ongoing training to refresher courses. They simplify and refine the procedure of cost assessment and certification.
